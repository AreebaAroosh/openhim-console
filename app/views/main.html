About the OpenHIM web console
The OpenHIM web console provides a central place to monitor and manage an Health Information Exchange (HIE). Here you can perform a number of function to make the management of an HIE as simple as possible. The core activities that can be performed are as follows:
  • Authorization and authentication management of application that connect to the HIE
  • Monitoring of the HIE performance
  • Inspecting logs of requests that have been make of the HIE infrastructure
  • Configure the channels that OpenHIM forward requests to
